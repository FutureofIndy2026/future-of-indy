About the Initiative

Future of Indy is a grassroots effort to engage Indianapolis residents in shaping the future of our city. We believe in fresh ideas, strong communities, and building infrastructure that serves everyone. Your voice helps drive meaningful change. None of this is achieved without strong, dedicated, and local hands-on leadership.
