About the Initiative

Future of Indy is an exploratory grassroots initiative designed to empower Indianapolis residents to share their vision for the future of our City. We are focused on gathering feedback around education, infrastructure, public safety, and community development to ensure a stronger, more vibrant Indianapolis for the next generation. It does not happen without focused, determined, hands-on and consistent energetic leadership.
